Note:
	You cant fully compile Script made in Pygames
	Just dont mess with anything or you cant doesnt matter, It will just stop working
	If you wanna silence music volume it goes to -1 like you know	
	Use the Start bat file to run it

Controls: 
	Player1:
		Arrow Keys to move and space bar to shoot

	Player2: 
		WASD Keys to move and shoot auto
	
	Options:
		P to Pause inside the game
		M to open Music menu
		Esc to quit

Addions:
	Inside the music folder you can add any music you want to play inside the game
	Just dont delete the original or anything it will stop working

Recommended settings:
			Music volume on 1 bar
			Game volume also on 1 bar or 0
